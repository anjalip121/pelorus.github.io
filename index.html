Pelorus Health helps people with T1D and their caregivers transition to independence.

Pelorus is a mobile appication currently in development. Connect with us to learn about our journey as we progress!

Remote Health Monitoring for T1D

Build and share a daily schedule with your caregiver including bolus reminders, time in range statistics, alarms, and create custom smart reminders. Emergency features include remote pinging, and an emergency letter.

Accessible Diabetes Education

Become a lifelong T1D learner with brief, realistic learning modules. Topics include: T1D basics, T1D management paths, Mindfulness, Life Skills, and more!

T1D Community Forums

While you build indepence on your T1D journey, build a community. Learn how to seek reliable health information sources and get the opportunity to share your own experiences.
