About Pelorus

Our Story

Anjali, the CEO and Co-founder of Pelorus Health, has a rare liver condition called Glycogen Storage Disease, a metabolic disease similar to diabetes. Anjali was inspired to build Pelorus as a tool to help children with chronic illness and their caregiver grow up to manage their conditions safely and independently. “Doctors would give me technical information and my parents were always very supportive, but I didn't feel I could talk to them about my day-to-day struggles,” she says. “Pelorus Health was born out of a desire to help patients connect.”
